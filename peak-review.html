My Peak Review:

Peak on the surface is a fun climbing game about working together with your friends to reach the peak of a very treacherous mountain. It might start this way, you might be having fun, helping your buddies climb by standing above them and offering a hand, feeding them when they are hungry and overall helping them out. It will not stay that way. Soon enough your friends will stop respecting that you need to bring Bing Bong with you. They will stop waiting up to help you up when you are low on stamina. They will feed you poisonous mushroom and they WILL eat you the moment your goofy little character turns into a big walking cooked rotisserie chicken!

*Ahem* Anyway, peak is a fun multiplayer game about climbing. It has a bunch of different gadgets like rope spools, canons, pitons, ballon and chain guns to help you and your friends climb. It aslo has a hunger and stamina system so you have to take care of yourself and make sure you have enough energy to make the climb and don't forget if you hurt yourself you're going to find it harder to climb. With perseverence and teamwork you will be able to reach the peak. The game is a masterclass in social game development, with the right mix of helpful ways friends can assist eachother and funny way friends can mess with eachother. I have played peak a bunch mainly with 2 friends and it has been a blast. I would highly recommend anyone with a friend group who enjoys working together and screwing eachother over to try it it!
